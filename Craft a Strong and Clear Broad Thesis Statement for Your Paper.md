## What is a Broad Thesis Statement?

A broad thesis statement is a general statement that provides a wide perspective on the topic you are discussing. It gives an overview of your argument, without narrowing it down to a single specific point. While it may not go into great detail, a broad thesis statement sets up the structure of your paper and invites the reader to explore the issue in-depth.
